What the pipeline does (target AUC > 0.90)

  • Leakage guard — flagged_for_review excluded from all features
  • 20+ feature groups — velocity (1h/6h/24h/7d), smoothed target encoding, frequency, time-since-last, network, amount percentile rank, V*/C*/D* with PCA
  • Logistic Regression — trained directly with sklearn (balanced weights)
  • 4 FLAML-tuned models — LightGBM · CatBoost · Random Forest · XGBoost · each 300s with metric=recall
  • Progressive saves — each model saved to DB immediately after training, champion updated in real-time
  • Champion scoring — Recall (50%) + PR-AUC (20%) + AUC-ROC (20%) + F1 (10%)
  • predictions.csv — auto-generated and updated by the best model
